🏀 How It Works

Each entrant picks 10 teams before the tournament begins. You earn points every time one of your teams wins a game. Points cannot be lost — every win adds to your total.

🧮 Scoring Formula

Points = Seed × Round Number. Higher seeds (bigger upsets) earn more points. Deeper runs earn more per win.
